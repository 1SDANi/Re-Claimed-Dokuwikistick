==== Turnabout ====
----

[[re_completed: Element]]: [[re_completed: element: Physical]]

Can [[re_completed: action: React]] using [[re_completed: reaction: Frozen Accessory]] for each ice block that is left

Ends [[re_completed: limits: Icy Ensemble]] once no more ice blocks are left
