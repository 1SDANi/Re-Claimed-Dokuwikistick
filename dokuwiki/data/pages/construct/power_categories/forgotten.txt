==== Forgotten ====
----

Forgotten are driven by a desire to protect

Forgotten have one ability

Forgotten have one domain

Forgotten can psychically control their domain

A Forgotten can pass from their Native World into their Other World

When a Forgotten dies, they are reborn into the Forgotten World

People and objects in the Known World are visible in the Forgotten World

People in the Known World are intangible to people in the Forgotten World

Objects in the Known World are unmovable by people in the Forgotten World
