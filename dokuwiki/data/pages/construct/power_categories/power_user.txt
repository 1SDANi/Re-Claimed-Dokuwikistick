==== Power User ====
----

Power Users can use their life force to augment their physical ability

Power Users can sense life force

Power Users can sense life force better the less distractions there are

Power Users generate more life force the better they understand their power

Power Users can accrue life force debt, wherein they borrow life force from their future

Life Force debt cannot be accrued unless the Power User is out of life force

Second Wind refers to the state of a Power User who is accruing life force debt

When a Power user stops accruing life force debt they enter a coma as their pass any life force they generate to their past self

Eternal Second Wind refers to the state of a Power User who understands their power so well that they generate life force faster than it is possible for them to expend it
