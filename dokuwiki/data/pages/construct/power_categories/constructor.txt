==== Constructor ====
----

Constructors are driven by a desire to grow

Constructors have one ability

Constructors have one weapon

Constructors can construct their ability into their weapon

Constructors can construct their ability into an astral projection

Constructors cannot use their ability on their own while their ability is constructed into a construct

Constructors can have their weapon use their ability while they are in physical contact with their weapon

Constructors can psychically have their astral projection use their ability

Constructors can psychically  control their astral projection as an extension of their body

Astral projection do not have senses

Astral projections are physically identical to their Constructor

Damage done to an astral projection is done to the Constructor
