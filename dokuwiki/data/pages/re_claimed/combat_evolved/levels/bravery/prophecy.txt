==== Prophecy ====
----

Scenario: The [[re_claimed: character: Prophet of Inquisition]]'s Divine Convoy has been spotted landing on the roof of a structure in a rainforest

Objective 1: Reach the [[re_claimed: maps: 04_Rainforest: Temple Exterior]]
  * Objective 1A: Sabotage [[re_claimed: team: Covenant]] [[re_claimed: vehicle: Weevil]]s
    * Reward: Ally reinforcements are available in O2
  * Objective 1B: Kill [[re_claimed: character: Jiralhanae Stalker]]s
    * Reward: More ally reinforcements in O2
  * Objective 1C: Kill a [[re_claimed: character: Sharqoi]]
    * Reward: [[re_claimed: character: Sharqoi]] do not spawn in O2

Twist: The Covenant have fully fortified the temple

Objective 2: Capture, interrogate, and kill the [[re_claimed: character: Prophet of Inquisition]]
  * Objective 2A: Sabotage the Divine Convoy
    * Reward: Weaker enemy reinforcements in [[re_claimed: combat_evolved: levels: bravery: Cartographer]]
  * Objective 2B: Kill the [[re_claimed: character: Sangheili Honor Guard]]
    * Reward: Fewer [[re_claimed: character: Sangheili Honor Guard]] spawns in [[re_claimed: combat_evolved: levels: bravery: Cartographer]]
  * Objective 2C: Capture a [[re_claimed: character: Huragok]]
     * Reward 2C: O2C automatically completed in [[re_claimed: combat_evolved: levels: bravery: Cartographer]]

Conclusion: The [[re_claimed: character: Prophet of Inquisition]] traveled to the temple to decipher holy [[re_claimed: character: team: Forerunner]] text divining the location of the [[re_claimed: levels: bravery: Cartographer: Silent Cartographer]]
