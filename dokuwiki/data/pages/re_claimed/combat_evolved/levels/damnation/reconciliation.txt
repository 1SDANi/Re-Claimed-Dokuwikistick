==== Reconciliation ====
----

Scenario: A Divine Convoy has been spotted docking in the Truth and Reconciliation, a ship [[re_claimed: character: Cortana]] disabled before abandoning the Autumn, if the ship is important enough for a [[re_claimed: character: Prophet]] to visit, [[re_claimed: character: Captain Keyes]] has to be onboard

Objective 1: Board the [[re_claimed: vehicle: Ket Class Cruiser]] Truth and Reconciliation
  * Objective 1A: Sabotage [[re_claimed: team: Covenant]] [[re_claimed: vehicle: Weevil]]s
    * Reward: Ally reinforcements are available in O2
  * Objective 1B: Secure landing zones for allies
    * Reward: More ally reinforcements in O2
  * Objective 1C: Capture [[re_claimed: team: Covenant]] fortifications
    * Reward: Less enemy reinforcements in O2

Twist: None

Objective 2: Rescue [[re_claimed: character: Captain Keyes]]
  * Objective 2A: Free [[re_claimed: team: UNSC]] POWs
    * Reward: More ally spawns in [[re_claimed: combat_evolved: levels: bravery: Prophecy]]
  * Objective 2B: Upload [[re_claimed: character: Cortana]] to the [[re_claimed: vehicles: Ket Class Cruiser: Bridge: Computer]]
    * Reward: Triggers [[re_claimed: Characters: Sharqoi]] spawns, also [[re_claimed: character: Sharqoi]] spawn in [[re_claimed: combat_evolved: levels: bravery: Prophecy]]
  * Objective 2C: Kill a [[re_claimed: character: Sangheili Zealot]]
    * Reward 2C: More ally spawns during [[re_claimed: combat_evolved: levels: Bravery]]

Conclusion: [[re_claimed: character: Captain Jacob Keyes]] is rescued and was interrogated by the [[re_claimed: character: Prophet of Inquisition]], kept rambling about the ring, seemed relieved that [[re_claimed: Characters: Captain Keyes]] didn't know "its true power", escaped with [[re_claimed: Characters: Captain Keyes]] in a [[re_claimed: vehicle: Phantom]]
